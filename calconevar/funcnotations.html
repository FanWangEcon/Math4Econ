Function Notations

When you read your first economic papers, perhaps one of the more confusing things is function notation. Economic models include potentially a lot of variables, and different functional notations are used by different authors based on personal preference. It's a choice you get to make, just make sure you use consistent function notation when you write down your models. And also state clearly at the beginning what your notations mean.
